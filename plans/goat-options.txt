GOAT SPRITE OPTIONS (v2 -- jgs-inspired)

Your feedback: previous options had the head in the wrong direction,
and I should look at Joan Stark's ("jgs") goat for scaling reference.

Her original side-view goat from 12/96 (20 lines, ~40 wide) is at the
bottom of this file for reference. Head is on the LEFT, curly horns up
top, body extends RIGHT, legs alternate, grazing arc reaches grass on
the right. This is the "right-facing" canonical form: goat faces the
direction it's walking; horns curl back from the face.

Scaling that down to fit in TUI cells (need <= 5 rows, <= 12 wide),
here are variants. Walking uses two frames with alternating legs.
Grazing has the head dipped down to grass. In every sprite below,
the goat faces RIGHT (its left-facing twin would be mirrored).
